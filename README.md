## 📖 Overview

The **Street Vendor Licensing Management System** is a full-stack digital governance platform designed to replace manual, paper-based processes for street vendor licensing with a modern, transparent, and efficient system.

It bridges the gap between **government/municipal authorities** and **street vendors**, enabling:
- Faster license approvals
- Fair and transparent zone allocation
- Digital record-keeping and compliance tracking
- Easy renewal and payment management

This system is particularly designed to be **inclusive** — supporting women-led small businesses and low-income vendors who rely on accessible digital tools.

## ✨ Key Features

### 👤 Vendor Management
- **Vendor Registration & Profile Management** — Easy onboarding with personal data and document upload
- **License Application & Approval Workflow** — Streamlined submission, review, and verification pipeline
- **Digital License Issuance** — Instant QR-coded licenses that are verifiable on the spot
- **License Renewal** — Automated expiry reminders with simple one-click renewal process

### ⚙️ Operational Efficiency
- **Vendor Location & Zone Management** — Interactive map-based allocation of designated vending zones
- **Fee & Payment Management** — Secure digital payment integration for all licensing fees
- **Complaint & Grievance Management** — Centralized portal for vendors and public to raise issues
- **Inspection & Compliance Monitoring** — Field officer tools to log inspections and flag violations
- **Notifications Module** — Real-time alerts for application updates, renewals, and announcements

---

## 🔄 System Workflow

```
1. Vendor Registration     →  Submit documents, create profile
2. Apply for License       →  Upload documents, pay application fee
3. Admin Review            →  Authorities verify and approve/reject
4. Digital License Issued  →  Vendor receives QR-coded digital license
5. Zone Assignment         →  Vendor is allocated a designated vending spot
6. Renewal                 →  Auto-reminders sent; vendor pays and renews
```
